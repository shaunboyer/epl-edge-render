How is Edge calculated?

Step 1 — No-Vig Probability: Strip the overround from each bookmaker to get their true implied probability for each outcome.

Step 2 — Consensus Model: Average no-vig probabilities across all tracked bookmakers — a wisdom-of-crowds estimate of true probability.

Step 3 — Home Advantage: Apply +2.5% to the home team based on EPL historical averages (~44% home win rate).

Step 4 — Edge: Edge % = (Model Prob − Break-even Prob) × 100. Positive = the odds offer more than you need to break even.

Step 5 — Line Movement: Odds are snapshotted to localStorage. Shifts indicate where sharp money is moving.
